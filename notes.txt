x86 Codegen Notes
=================

1) You must use the REX prefix to do 64-bit instructions.
     REX byte -> 0100 WRXB

     High 4 bits are set to 4.

       W - Upgrade operand size to 64-bit
       R - Access registers R8-R15
       X - Extends SIB.index field to 4 bits
       B - Extends ModRM.r/m, SIB.base or opcode register field to 4 bits.

2) 64-bit registers are supersets of 32-bit registers,
   eg. EAX is the low 32-bits of RAX (64-bit)

3) Parameter passing.
     Parameters: RDI, RSI, RDX, RCX, R8, R9
     Return:     RAX (integers), XMM0 (FP)

4) The stack must be 16-byte aligned before a CALL.
